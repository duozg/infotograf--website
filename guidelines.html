Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Infotograf is a place for sharing photos and connecting with others. These guidelines exist to keep our community safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone. By using Infotograf, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Share Thoughtfully

  • Only post photos and content that you have the right to share
  • Respect the privacy of others — don't post photos of people without their consent
  • Give credit when sharing work inspired by others

Be Respectful

  • Treat others the way you want to be treated
  • Disagreement is fine; personal attacks, bullying, and harassment are not
  • Don't use slurs, hate speech, or language intended to demean others based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic

No Harassment or Bullying

We take harassment seriously. This includes:

  • Repeatedly sending unwanted messages or comments
  • Threatening violence or harm against any person
  • Sharing someone's private information without consent (doxxing)
  • Coordinated attacks against individuals
  • Creating accounts to evade blocks or bans

No Harmful or Dangerous Content

  • Do not post content that promotes or glorifies self-harm or suicide
  • Do not post content that encourages dangerous activities
  • Do not post content that promotes violence or terrorism

No Sexually Explicit Content

  • Nudity and sexually explicit content are not allowed
  • Content depicting or promoting the sexual exploitation of minors is strictly prohibited and will be reported to authorities

No Spam or Manipulation

  • Don't use bots or automated tools to interact with the service
  • Don't create fake accounts or impersonate others
  • Don't post repetitive, misleading, or deceptive content
  • Don't use Infotograf to sell products, advertise, or solicit users

No Illegal Activity

  • Do not use Infotograf for any illegal purpose
  • Do not post content that infringes on intellectual property rights
  • Do not facilitate or promote illegal transactions

Reporting Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it:

  • Report a post: Tap the menu on any post and select "Report"
  • Report a user: Go to their profile, tap the menu, and select "Report"
  • Block a user: You can block anyone to prevent them from seeing your content or contacting you
  • Contact us: Email support@infotograf.com for urgent concerns

Enforcement

We review all reports and take action based on the severity of the violation:

  • Content removal: Posts or comments that violate these guidelines will be removed
  • Warning: First-time or minor violations may result in a warning
  • Temporary suspension: Repeated violations may result in a temporary account suspension
  • Permanent ban: Severe or repeated violations will result in permanent account termination

We reserve the right to remove any content and suspend or terminate any account at our discretion, without prior notice.

Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you may contact us at support@infotograf.com to request a review.
