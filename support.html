Frequently Asked Questions

What is Infotograf?

Infotograf is a photo-sharing app inspired by the golden era of Instagram (2012–2015). It features a chronological feed, classic photo filters, and no algorithmic content. Just photos from the people you follow, in the order they posted them.

Is Infotograf free?

Yes. Infotograf is free to download and use. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no premium tiers.

Why is my feed chronological?

Because we believe you should decide what you see, not an algorithm. Your feed shows posts from people you follow, sorted by when they were shared — newest first. Always.

How do I delete my account?

Open Infotograf, go to your Profile tab, tap the settings icon, then scroll down to "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data including posts, comments, likes, and messages.

Can I use Infotograf on Android?

Infotograf is currently available only on iOS. We may consider other platforms in the future.

How do I report a problem or abusive content?

You can report posts or users directly in the app by tapping the menu on any post or profile and selecting "Report." For other issues, email us at support@infotograf.com.

What photo filters are available?

Infotograf includes nine classic filters: Clarendon, Gingham, Moon, Lark, Reyes, Juno, Slumber, Ludwig, and Aden. Plus the original "Normal" (no filter) option. Each filter is built with Core Image for native-quality processing.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Go to your Profile, tap the settings icon, then select "Appearance." You can choose between System (follows your device setting), Light, or Dark.
