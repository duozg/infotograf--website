Questions

Why does verification cost money?

Every request is manually reviewed by a real person. The $4.99 covers the time and helps filter spam. It's a one-time cost — you'll never be charged again.

What if my verification is denied?

You'll receive a full automatic refund through Apple. We'll let you know why so you can reapply.

What happens to my ID photo?

Your selfie and any documents are only viewed during review. They are permanently deleted once approved or denied.

Can I lose my verified badge?

Only if you violate community guidelines, change your username, or if the identity was fraudulent. Normal use means you keep it forever.

Does the badge appear across the Fediverse?

The badge is visible within Infotograf and attached to your ActivityPub profile. Other Fediverse apps may display it depending on their implementation.

Do I need to be famous to get verified?

No. Verification on Infotograf is a safety feature, not a status symbol. Anyone can verify — it means you proved you're a real person.

Ready to verify?

Download Infotograf and tap "Get Verified" in your profile settings. The whole process takes about two minutes.
