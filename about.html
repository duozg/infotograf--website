Introducing

Infotograf

Photo sharing, the way it used to be. No algorithm, no reels, no shopping — just you, your camera, and the people you care about.

Your feed, your order

Chronological Feed

No algorithm decides what you see. Your feed shows posts from the people you follow, in the order they were shared. Scroll through photos, not recommendations.

Talk to your people

Direct Messaging

Real-time conversations with typing indicators, emoji reactions, and voice messages. No read receipts pressure — just natural conversations with the people who matter.

Tell the whole story

Multi-Photo Posts

Share up to five photos in a single post. Swipe through carousels of your best moments — a day at the pool, a road trip with friends, or just your morning coffee from every angle.

Beyond one app

Fediverse

Connect with Mastodon, Pixelfed, and the wider Fediverse. Your posts reach people across platforms, and they can follow you — without needing an Infotograf account.

You’re in control

Privacy & Safety

Go private so only approved followers see your photos. Report content that doesn’t belong. No data harvesting, no ad targeting — your photos are yours.
